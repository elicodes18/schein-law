Avoid These 5 Common Visa Mistakes

  1. Submitting documents without Hague apostille or certified translations.
  2. Letting temporary visas lapse before filing renewals.
  3. Incorrectly documenting proof of funds or local domicile.
  4. Assuming family members can apply without synchronized timelines.
  5. Skipping legal review of contracts that support your residency proof.
