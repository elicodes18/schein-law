Quick Answers

Do Russians need to speak Spanish for citizenship?

No, Argentina's citizenship process (via residency) does not currently have a mandatory language or history exam, which is a significant advantage.

Can I bring my family?

Yes. We specialize in family relocation. Spouses and children can typically apply for residency along with the primary applicant.

What’s the fastest citizenship route?

The standard path is 2 years of continuous residency. Dr. Schein ensures your application is filed correctly to avoid any delays in this process.
